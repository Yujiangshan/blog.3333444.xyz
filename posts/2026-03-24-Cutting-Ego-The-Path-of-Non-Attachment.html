⚔️ Cutting Ego: The Path of Non-Attachment

2026 · 03 · 24  ·  禅宗智慧 · 断除烦恼 · 心灵成长

True practitioners are like swordsmen of the spirit. The sword of Prajna in their hands is not for killing living beings, but for severing the root of Samsara within the mind.

Origin of Prajna: Severing Delusion

At the beginning of practice, the most difficult things to handle are greed, anger, and ignorance. If one holds a sword but does not aim at the thief, they will be harmed. This means wisdom must be applied directly to the ego and the 'stealing mind'.

To 'kill one in ten steps' is a precise strike against wandering thoughts. Every moment a defilement arises is an opportunity for enlightenment. By cutting the continuity of thought, the false self vanishes and the chains of rebirth break.

Method of Contemplation: Walking Without Trace

When delusions are severed, the mind enters a state of 'a thousand miles without a trace'. This is not escaping the world, but maintaining absolute clarity. Like a wild goose crossing the sky, the water reflects the shadow without a heart to retain it.

The practice lies in the 'cessation of words and thoughts'. Engage fully in tasks, then let go immediately. Do not dwell on past gains or future worries; this present moment is eternal stillness. This requires constant vigilance like a cat catching a mouse.

Realization and Dedication: The Joy of Emptiness

When all attachments dissolve, the practitioner awakens from the dream of suffering. You will find that essentially nothing exists in the fruit of Buddhahood. This peace is the true joy beyond worldly pleasure.

May this sword of wisdom break all attachments and ignorance in the world. No matter the circumstances, let the mind be like the sun in the sky, illuminating everything without being stained by anything. Realize the ultimate freedom of space.

世人皆惊叹剑客的潇洒，却不知真正的修行者亦是剑客。手中的般若利剑不为杀生，只为在方寸之间斩断轮回的根源。

般若缘起：慧剑斩断妄念根源

修行之初，最难对治的是绵延不绝的贪嗔痴。笔记中提到，若执利剑而不拟贼，反受其害。这意味着智慧不能只停留在口头，而要实实在在地对准心中的“我执”与“偷心”。

所谓“十步杀一人”，实则是对妄念的精准狙击。每一个烦恼生起的瞬间，都是证悟的契机。若能迅捷斩断相续，那虚妄的“自我”便无处遁形，轮回的锁链亦随之断裂。

唯有看破“见闻觉知”的虚幻，才能不被外境所转。我们往往认贼为子，将起伏的念头当成真实。唯有运用般若之利刃，方能拨云见日，显发本具的清净自性。

观照法门：直下无心千里独行

当妄念被斩断，心便进入了“千里不留行”的状态。这并非逃避世间，而是在繁忙的生活中保持一份绝对的清明。如雁过长空，影沉寒水，雁无遗踪之意，水无留影之心。

具体的用功处，在于“言语道断，心行处灭”。做事时全身心投入，不生二心；事毕即放下，不留余味。不计较过去的得失，不忧虑未来的变幻，当下这一刻即是永恒的寂静。

这需要如猫捕鼠般的警觉与专注。在初、中、后夜，于行、住、坐、卧，始终护持这颗不着相的心。如钻木取火，不可中途停息，唯有恒久的常习不废，方能见证自性的光辉。

证悟回向：虚通寂静证得余韵

当所有的攀缘与执着悉数消散，修行者将迎来彻底的翻转。你会发现，原来佛果之上本无一物，所谓的辛勤历劫，不过是一场大梦的觉醒。此时的安乐，是超越世俗苦乐的真常之乐。

回向众生，愿以此慧剑，破除世间一切情执与无明。无论身处何境，皆能如大日悬空，照见万物而不与万物粘着。于万丈红尘中，证得一份如虚空般无痕的自在。
