🌌 Returning to the Void: The Essence of Sunyata

2026 · 03 · 25  ·  空性 · 金刚经 · 禅修

Emptiness is not a void, but the original face of reality revealed once attachments are severed. Many practitioners get trapped by the terminology of "void," missing the instant of liberation found in letting go of appearances.

Piercing the Fog

True vacuum is the clarity of a mind no longer deluded by phenomena. When all dualities vanish, the inherent purity of our nature manifests naturally. We must perceive that all dharmas are temporary labels to become free from external disturbances.

This stillness is the foundation of wisdom, not desolation. It grants life a weightless tension, allowing one to maintain innate freedom even within the chaotic world. By understanding the vacuum, we find the courage to let go.

Beyond All Appearances

In true Sunyata, "Buddha" and "sentient beings" are merely labels. If one harbors a "Buddha-state" to achieve, they remain imprisoned by the mind of gain and loss. Our nature is originally complete and requires no external seeking.

Letting go of attachment to states is the most critical step in practice. Only when the heart of gain and loss dissolves into emptiness can the radiance of Dharma-nature break through. This is the path to non-attachment.

Practice in Every Thought

Spiritual practice resides in the rising and falling of every single thought. When we cease to cling at the moment a thought occurs, we are walking within the Tathagata vacuum. Moving through this dream-like world with a heart as vast as space is the ultimate destination of great freedom.

---

空性并非虚无，而是破除执着后显现的本来面目。许多行者在求法路上常被“空”的名相所困，却不知解脱就在离相的一瞬。

拨开迷雾见真空

真空并非顽空，而是行者在彻底了悟一切虚妄后，内心生起的一种不被现象迷惑的清净。当所有的执着与二元对立消失，众生本具的清净自性才会自然显现。

我们习惯于在名相中寻找依靠，却忽略了万法本自空寂的本质。若能洞察万法皆是假立，心自然不再受外境的纷扰与束缚。

这种空寂是智慧的底色，而非断灭的荒凉。它赋予生命一种轻盈的张力，让行者在滚滚红尘中也能保持一份本然的自在。

离相方能得自在

真正的真空境界中没有高低贵贱之分，佛与众生皆是为了指月而设的假名。若心中仍存有一个可供追求的“如来境界”，则依然困于得失的牢笼。

如经中所云，真说法者无说无示，听法者亦无闻无得。这种“无得”并非贫乏，而是因为自性本来圆满，无需向外求索。

放下对境界的执着，是实修中最难也最关键的一步。只有当得失心消融在空性中，法性的光辉才能从虚妄的相中透脱而出。

在动念处修无住

修行不在深山老林，而在每一次念头的生灭之间。当我们在起心动念处不再粘着，不再随境流转，便是行在如来的真空中。这种不粘着的力量能让我们从万法的束缚中解脱。在如梦如幻的世间穿行，心如虚空般广博而无碍，这便是大自在的终极归归宿。
